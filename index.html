who is jarissa?

Well it depends on who you ask but if you ask me she's a jack of many trades! I pride myself in being openminded and enjoy trying new things which is how I ended up here at the MS coding academy with the greatest instructor to ever LIVEEEE, Hebert Brown! I grew up in the myspace days where webpage customization was a must and if you didn't have the perfect song playing on your profile then you were definitely doing it wrong! Computers have always been something I was passionate about, it would be the main thing on my Christmas list each year. I noticed everytime I would do things for fun or just personal use it would be technical related where I had to use some form of technology so this lead me down the path of coding. While looking for a career where I am always learning and more than likely will never have the same work day, I found that coding is a great place to start!
